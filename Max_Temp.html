Latitude vs. Max Temperature

As anticipated, the temperatures have a noticable rise as one approaches the Equator (0 deg lat) from either the north or south. It is interesting to note the temperatures in the southern hemisphere are, as a whole, are warmer than temperatures collected in northern latitudes. This may be due having gathered data during the winter season in the northern hemisphere - the time at which the southern hemisphere is experiencing summer.

It may also be noted that there were significantly fewer cities reporting data in the southern henisphere. This could be either due to random chance of how cities were selected, or the fact that approximately 80.9% of all land on Earth is in the northern hemisphere.
